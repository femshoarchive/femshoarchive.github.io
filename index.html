FAQ

Q: Why did you make this site?

A: When I initialy started making this site I didn't know Shounic has a stream archive playlist and when I did, throwing everything out would've been a waste of effort so fuck it. And I also think I did a pretty good job designing everything.

Q: Why aren't you just using YouTube embeds?

A: Basically same thing as the last anwser, had few streams downloaded on my phone since I didn't know about that playlist and that would've also been a waste of effort. "But what if Shounic decides to delete these VODs"... Yeah, fuck it.

Q: Why aren't you using [Framework Name Here] / Why is your code a fucking mess?

A: I'm not a web developer, alright? I do not need any bloat or JavaScript where it's not necesary for such a simple site. I made this site for fun and not to make money.
